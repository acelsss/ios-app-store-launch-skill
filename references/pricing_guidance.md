# Pricing Guidance

Use this reference when a user asks how to price an iOS app.

## Focused Utility Apps

Default recommendation:

- Start with one-time paid purchase.
- Use a low-friction launch price, often near the local equivalent of USD 1.99.
- Use Apple automatic price conversion across storefronts first.
- Avoid country-specific manual pricing until there is real conversion, refund, and review data.

## When To Consider Subscription

Subscriptions fit better when the app has:

- Ongoing server costs.
- Continual content updates.
- Cloud sync or collaboration.
- Professional recurring usage.

## Paid App Setup

Remind the user that paid apps require:

- Paid Apps Agreement.
- Tax setup.
- Banking setup.

If those are incomplete, App Store Connect may only allow free pricing.
